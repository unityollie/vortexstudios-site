Our Vision:

At Vortex Studios, we strive to break down the traditional barriers that prevent young and talented individuals from entering the IT and game development industries. Our goal is to offer a platform where IT students, with little or no experience, can develop real-world skills, build portfolios, and begin their careers in a supportive and growth-driven environment.

What are we?

Vortex Studios is a upcoming game development company with a mission to create innovative gaming experiences while offering unique opportunities to aspiring IT professionals. At Vortex, we believe that no matter your talent or skill set you should be accepted into exploring what your best interests are and what you aspire to be doing. Vortex Studios aims to provide work experience to IT students and entry-level professionals who face the frustrating barrier of needing "experience" when trying to enter the industry.

Who We Are:

We are a dedicated team of students committed to creating and expanding future opportunities for other students.
